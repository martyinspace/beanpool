Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. Zero Knowledge & Decentralization

BeanPool is a peer-to-peer (P2P) localized protocol natively bypassing centralized tracking silos. Our systems are exclusively mathematically verifiable, functioning without a central corporate authority reading your metadata. All cryptographic keypairs (your Ed25519 identity) are generated and stored strictly within your physical mobile device's Secure Enclave structurally identically to App Store security standards. We do not hold a master database copy of your identity, and we literally cannot recover your account if you lose your phone and have no physical backup.

2. Data Collection Restrictions

We do not capture analytical telemetry, behavior-tracking metrics, or third-party marketing metadata natively. Your application physically communicates locally with community Nodes that you explicitly join and trust. Any marketplace "Needs" or "Offers" you create are broadcast publicly to the specific community ledger tied securely to your cryptographic callsign seamlessly mapping data explicitly on an opt-in basis exclusively.

3. Account Eradication (Apple Guideline 5.1.1.v)

You inherently possess full control to securely erase your physical identity natively at any point via the Settings -> Delete Account / WIPE application dashboard exclusively. Because the core system is heavily decentralized and encrypts its primary data blocks offline originally, deleting your local hardware keys instantly renders all your historical transactions cryptographically untamperable and your physical account permanently inaccessible bypassing centralized retention delays.

4. Moderation Metrics & UGC

Because the platform enables users to broadcast publicly generated Needs and Offers mapping physical neighborhood networks natively, we enforce completely strict automated moderation vectors internally. The client allows any verified participant to formally sever, block, and flag cryptographically abusive material instantaneously—which transmits verified cryptographic signatures securely back to the community administrators securely mapping moderation safety compliance flawlessly against App Store policies.
