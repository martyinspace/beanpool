## 2026-05-04 - Adding aria attributes to icon-only buttons\n**Learning:** In the @beanpool/pwa app, icon-only buttons frequently use a `title` attribute for tooltips but miss necessary `aria-label` and state attributes (`aria-pressed`/`aria-expanded`) for screen readers.\n**Action:** When creating or modifying icon-only buttons, ensure `aria-label` is used alongside `aria-pressed` or `aria-expanded` for toggle buttons to properly convey their state and purpose to assistive technologies.
